Overview

HomeEase is a service provider born out of passion to help undergraduates live a better life while in school. Having being pioneered by people who were(are) undergraduates, we understand the hardship student face while in search of a place to stay in school, finding good roommates and running their businesses effectively. We at Home Ease are interested in helping students focus on their Academics and reduce their stress level which leads them to achieving excellence and greatness.

Mission

At HomeEase, our mission is to empower undergraduate students by providing them with an exceptional service that prioritizes their comfort and well-being. We strive to eliminate the stress of finding suitable accommodation/roommates and providing a platform where students can do their business with less stress while promoting academic focus. We are committed to helping students achieve their full potential by delivering excellence and unmatched service that enhances their college experience.

Vision

"At HomeEase, our vision is to revolutionize the student living experience by offering seamless and stress-free solutions that enhance academic performance, personal growth, and overall well-being. We aspire to be the go-to service provider for undergraduates seeking comfortable, affordable, and safe living arrangements, reliable business support, and proven roommate-matching solutions. Our goal is to empower students with the tools they need to achieve greatness and succeed in their academic and personal journeys."

Goals & Objectives

  1. To provide quality housing accommodations for undergraduate students.
  2. To facilitate roommate matching for our clients.
  3. To offer effective business management services for student-run businesses.
  4. To create a stress-free living environment for our clients.
  5. To prioritize the academic success of our clients.
  6. To establish a strong reputation as a trusted and reliable service provider for undergraduate students.

Core Values

  • We work with innovative people.
  • Unconventional thinking is our lifeblood.
  • Our teams are energetic and fun filled.
  • Sustainable long term growth is key to our success.
  • Home Ease cares about and supports our students.
