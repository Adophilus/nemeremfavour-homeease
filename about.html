Overview

Home Ease provides basic services to university students for free. It offers easy accommodation, fast roommate matching, frequently asked questions and mini-ecommerce.

About Us

At Home Ease, we want to help Nigerian Students. Whatever you wish with our innovative products. We want to see our students succeed and a life of ease which defines their adult life.

Mission

Our mission is to provide services and products to students at their comfort; making life easier for Nigerian Students.

Vision

To redefine the belief oh high institutional study to be easy, worthwhile and life-changing.

Goals & Objectives

  • To reach out to all high institutions both public and private in Nigeria offering a little help to our students.
  • To reduce the mortality rate at higher institutions which are based mainly on stress.
  • To contribute to the success stories of our students.

Core Values

  • We work with innovative people.
  • Unconventional thinking is our lifeblood.
  • Our teams are energetic and fun filled.
  • Sustainable long term growth is key to our success.
  • Home Ease cares about and supports our students.
